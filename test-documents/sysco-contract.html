FOOD SERVICE SUPPLY AGREEMENT
1. PARTIES & EFFECTIVE DATE
This Food Service Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024 ("Effective Date") between:
  • VENDOR: Sysco Food Services of Columbus, LLC, an Ohio limited liability company ("Sysco" or "Supplier")
  • CUSTOMER: Sunset Manor Nursing Home, 450 Healthcare Blvd, Gahanna, OH 43230 ("Customer" or "Facility")
2. TERM & RENEWAL
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of twelve (12) months, expiring on December 31, 2024. The Agreement will automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration date.
3. PRODUCTS & PRICING
Sysco agrees to supply Customer with food products, beverages, and related supplies as ordered. Pricing is based on the following structure:
Product Category Unit Price Unit Minimum Order
Fresh Vegetables - Mixed Cases $42.00 per case 10 cases
Protein - Ground Beef (80/20) $125.00 per case 5 cases
Dairy - Milk 2% Gallon Cases $34.50 per case 8 cases
Bread - Whole Wheat Loaves $2.75 per loaf 20 loaves
Volume Discounts:
  • Orders exceeding $5,000 per month: 10% discount
  • Orders exceeding $10,000 per month: 15% discount
  • Bulk orders of 500+ units (same item): Additional 5% discount
4. PAYMENT TERMS
  • Payment Terms: Net 30 Days from invoice date
  • Early Payment Discount: 2% if paid within 10 days
  • Late Payment Fee: 1.5% per month on overdue balances after 30 days
  • Invoices will be sent via email to: billing@sunsetmanornursing.com
5. TAX & SHIPPING
  • Sales Tax: Applicable at the prevailing Ohio state and local rate (8.5% current rate). Tax-exempt customers must provide valid certificate.
  • Delivery: Free delivery on orders over $500. Orders under $500 incur a $35 delivery fee.
  • Delivery Schedule: Tuesday and Friday, 6:00 AM - 10:00 AM
6. PRICE ADJUSTMENTS
Pricing is guaranteed for the first six (6) months of this Agreement. After this period, Sysco may adjust prices due to market conditions, provided that:
  • Customer receives 60 days written notice of any price increase
  • Price increases shall not exceed 5% annually
  • Customer may terminate this Agreement without penalty if price increase exceeds 5%
7. QUALITY GUARANTEE
Sysco guarantees 100% product quality. Any defective, spoiled, or incorrectly shipped items will be replaced or fully refunded within 24 hours of notification. Customer must report quality issues within 48 hours of delivery.
8. RETURNS & RESTOCKING
  • Defective products: Full credit, no restocking fee
  • Non-defective returns: 15% restocking fee applies
  • Returns must be authorized within 7 days of delivery
9. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice. Immediate termination is permitted in cases of material breach, bankruptcy, or force majeure events lasting longer than 30 days.
